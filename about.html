About Us

A brief description of what we do.

The Corporate Counsel is an approved compliance and risk management practice founded in 2005. We provide specialist services to a select reputable group of financial services clients, including private equity funds, hedge funds, collective investment schemes and general advisory businesses.

Our services include the following;

We assist with applications to be approved as a financial services provider in terms of the Financial Advisory and Intermediary Services Act ("FAIS Act") and applying for any applicable exemptions.

We assist with applications to be licensed as an Over the Counter Derivative Provider.

We act as an external compliance officer in terms of the FAIS Act, the Financial Intelligence Centre Act and other applicable financial services legislation.

We assist with applicable policies such as business continuity plans, conflict of Interest , information barrier policies and anti money-laundering risk management and compliance plans.

We assist with preparation for on-site visits by the FSCA and other supervisory bodies.

We provide training to candidates in respect various financial services legislation including preparation for Regulatory Exams.
